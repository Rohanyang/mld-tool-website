WORKSHOP
OVERVIEW

Our facility spans 12,000 square meters, strategically divided into high-precision machining, assembly, tryout, and quality inspection zones.

Designed for efficiency, the workshop features overhead cranes with up to 50T capacity, ensuring safe handling of large automotive panels and transfer dies.

厂区占地 12,000 平方米，合理划分为精密加工、装配、试模及质检区域。配备 50 吨行车，确保大型汽车覆盖件模具的安全周转。
